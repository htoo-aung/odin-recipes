Lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe layers rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and velvety pasta sheets, topped with golden, bubbling cheese. A hearty and comforting dish, perfect for family gatherings or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • Sauce

    • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
    • 2 medium red onions, finely diced
    • 2 large cloves minced garlic
    • 8 ounces pancetta, diced
    • Salt and freshly ground pepper
    • 1½ cups good red wine, preferably Italian
    • 2 28-ounce cans Italian plum tomatoes
    • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
    • ¾ pound ground sirloin
    • ¼ cup freshly grated pecorino Romano
    • 2 eggs
    • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, washed and dried
    • 2 large whole gloves garlic
    • ½ cup flour
    • 1 pound Italian sausage, a mix of hot and sweet

  • Lasagna

    • 1 15-ounce container ricotta cheese
    • 2 eggs
    • 2 cups freshly grated pecorino Romano
    • ½ cup chopped parsley
    • 1 pound mozzarella, grated
    • 16 sheets fresh lasagna noodles, preferably Antica Pasteria

Steps

  1. Make the Sauce: In a Dutch oven, sauté onions, garlic, and pancetta in oil. Season with salt and pepper, add wine and reduce. Add crushed tomatoes, their juice, tomato paste, and water. Simmer for 1 hour.
  2. Prepare Meatballs: Mix sirloin, cheese, eggs, chopped parsley, and garlic. Season well and form into meatballs.
  3. Cook Meatballs: Brown the meatballs in a skillet and transfer to the sauce.
  4. Brown Sausages: In another skillet, brown sausages then add to the sauce. Simmer for 1½ hours.
  5. Prepare Cheese Mixture: Mix ricotta, eggs, pecorino Romano, parsley, and most of the mozzarella. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Assemble Lasagna: Layer sauce, noodles, meat (chopped meatballs and sausage), and cheese mixture in a lasagna pan. Repeat for 2 more layers. Top with noodles, remaining sauce, and reserved mozzarella. Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes. Let it stand for 10 minutes before serving.
