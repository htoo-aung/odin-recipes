Hawaiian Pizza

You can't beat this salty/sweet combination of savory ham and bacon along with juicy pineapple. (And we all know how well melty mozzarella cheese pairs with ham and pineapple, it's a match made in heaven!)

Ingredients

  • Pizza

    • 1 lb. pizza dough, at room temp
    • ½ cup pizza sauce
    • ½ cup sliced or diced cooked ham
    • 8 oz. pineapple chunks
    • 4 bacon strips
    • 2 cups mozzarella cheese

  • Garlic Butter Parmesan Crust

    • 1 tablespoon melted butter
    • 1 teaspoon Parmesan cheese
    • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder

  • To Garnish

    • Red Pepper Flakes
    • Parsley
    • Oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450° F. Note: If you have a pizza stone, put it in the oven while the oven reheats for more even cooking and a browner, crispier crust.
  2. Slice each pineapple chunk into 2 thinner slices and place them in between paper towels. They contain a lot of moisture, so we want to absorb the excess juice before adding them to the pizza.
  3. Spread the dough into an even 12-inch circle. Poke holes in it with a fork to prevent the crust from bubbling up in the oven.
  4. Top with pizza sauce, then with the mozzarella. Add the ham, pineapple and bacon.
  5. Bake for 13 minutes and remove from the oven.
  6. Combine the melted butter, Parmesan cheese, and garlic powder. Brush it over the crust and return it to the oven.
  7. Bake for 2-3 more minutes, or until the crust begins to brown.
  8. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes, parsley, and oregano. Slice and serve!
